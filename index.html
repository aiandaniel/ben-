About Ben 10

Ben Tennyson, a young boy with a mysterious watch-like device known as the Omnitrix, has the power to transform into a variety of alien creatures. Each alien has its unique abilities, from super strength to heat control. Join Ben as he faces off against formidable enemies and learns the true meaning of heroism.
